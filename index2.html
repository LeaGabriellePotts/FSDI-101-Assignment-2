Lea P

Kiteboarding

Lea P is a kiteboarding pioneer, who has been a leader in the transformation of the sport since 2001. Lea was serving in the military and stationed in Virgnina Beach, VA ref. wiki page when she was one of the first women to excel in kiteboarding. Her training began with Matt Nuzzo, Lew Simmonds, Lucie Nuzzo, and Trip Forman in Cape Hatteras, North Carolina.

Real Watersports

Real Watersports began in 2001 and created their trademark camp Zero To Hero after their training sessions with Lea 2001. They have since become the premier training facililty in the world for kiteboarding.
